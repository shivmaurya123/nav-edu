MBBS IN RUSSIA FOR INDIAN STUDENTS Studying internationally has always been an alluring preference of Indian students. Russia has been well acknowledged for its medical education since ages. Medical education in India is not only very expensive but also the competition of grabbing good college is really tough. Russian medical education is believed to be one of the best amongst the medical institutions of the world. Russia has an excellent history of exporting very good doctors across the globe. Russian medical colleges have an affordable fees structure and other expenses are very economic. Therefore, pursuing MBBS in Russia therefore grabs the first place when it comes to selecting medical universities for Indian students. Outlines of MBBS in Russia Russia has more than 70 medical colleges/universities which are known for their excellent education standards. Out of these medical universities more than 30 universities hold positions in top 100 rankings of World medical universities. All of these universities have reasonably low fees and very good standards of education. Syllabus or curriculum of medical education in Russia is very similar to the syllabus of Indian medical colleges that too in English medium. MBBS in Russia is around 5-6 years course with average total fees of 16-20 lakh. Intake of students in Russian medical universities takes place in the months of February and September. Benefits of Studying in Russia MBBS in Russia has numerous benefits like low finances, 100% visa surety, easy living lifestyle and most important being the best medical educational hub. Indian medical aspirants face a general issue of finances when they think of MBBS from India, secondly the competition level in the field of medical entrance is very high. These reasons are pretty enough for the students to move abroad for higher studies. MBBS in Russia is very cost effective because a good amount of subsidy in fees is provided by the Indian government also. Moreover, good political relations between the two countries make Indian students more comfortable in Russia. It is easier to get a student visa for higher studies in Russia than for other European countries. Medical universities in Russia There are around 70-80 medical universities in Russia and most famous universities amongst them are: · Bashkir State Medical university · Crimea state medical university · Kazan federal university · Volgograd state medical university · Orel state medical university · Belgorod state medical university Almost all universities are approved by the Indian Medical Council and World Health Organization and other important governing bodies. Russian medical universities have outstanding infrastructure and they provide the world's best medical education. The percentage of graduated students from Russia clearing the MCI screening exam is very high. This fact proves that the teaching & learning methods of Russian medical universities are excellent. Russian medical universities are known to have the best laboratories and technical facilities. The teachers are well qualified doctors or PhD holders. These universities provide good internship facilities also. Some of the universities have collaboration with top US medical colleges which enables students to take part in research activities in US medical universities. The field of research and development in Russia is astounding, therefore, Russian MBBS graduates are counted among the best medical practitioners. Eligibility Criteria of admission Studying MBBS in Russia is always on the priority list of those students who want to study abroad for MBBS. The minimum eligibility for pursuing MBBS in Russia is 50% marks in senior secondary school and the compulsory subjects in 10+2 are Biology, Physics & Chemistry. Although, some relaxation has been given to SC/ST/OBC candidates in terms of percentage of marks and age. All the students applying for Russian medical universities must have qualified for the NEET exam. No university or college requires English eligibility test i.e. IELTS OR TOEFL. Living facilities Because of the higher number of Indian students in Russia, the accommodation and food facilities are comforting and as per taste of Indian students. There is a large number of Indian students studying in Russia due to which the hostel and mess arrangements are done in a way to make Indian students feel like home. Hostels are fully furnished and veg/non veg both servings are done in a mess. The daily and other miscellaneous expenses are very feasible and low. Admission procedure The admission process for MBBS in Russia is very unambiguous. Candidates need to fill an application form and have to submit scanned copies of mark sheets of 10th and 12th. Some universities ask for registration fee while some have no registration fees. After filling the primary application form, candidates will receive confirmation mail by the respective university. Admission letter (if selected) will be issued to candidates within two weeks thereby securing your seat in the university. This selection is solely based on a first come first serve pattern. In the next step, the candidate needs to apply for a visa invitation letter which will be issued to the candidate by the Ministry of Foreign affairs of the Russian federation. It will take around 1 month of time. Once you get this, then you will be able to apply for a visa from the Russian embassy. After successfully getting a visa you will get sufficient information about your traveling details (date) and all the original documents including passport will be returned to you. Documents required: · Mark sheets/ certificate of 10th & 12th. · NEET score card · 6-8 colored passport size photographs · ID proof (Adhar card/PAN/Driving license) · Passport All the above documents should be in a scanned medium. Early application is advisable to students due to the large number of applicants and heavy rush on university websites. Future prospects of graduating students from Russia Russia is considered as the best alternative to pursue MBBS at low cost. Over the last many years, several hundred students have taken the degree of MBBS in Russia. Their precious feedback is the backbone of the basis of considering Russia for MBBS by the Indian students. For a doctor who possesses a degree of MBBS from Russia/or any other country, requires qualifying the exam conducted by Medical Council of India to practice in India. The statistics of students returning from Russia clearing this exam is highest as compared to the number of students passing MBBS from the rest of the other countries. MBBS in Russia is commonly known as MD (Doctor of Medicine) which is recognized as MBBS in India. The add on advantage of pursuing MBBS in Russia is that this degree is fully valid across the world MBBS in abroad has opened the doors of becoming a doctor for those who lags behind due to financial reasons or because of a tough and neck to neck competition in Indian medical entrance exam. In past few years, the trend of studying MBBS in abroad has revolutionized the cut-throat competition in the medical field. The number of students pursuing MBBS in abroad is increasing with each passing year. There are lots of options available for MBBS in abroad and choosing the best out of them is very difficult. MBBS in Russia is the oldest and most trustable option available. It is highly advisable to all the medical aspirants who wish to explore the world and are ready to have a world recognized degree to pursue MBBS in Russia.
